***THIS DATA PACK IS FOR MINECRAFT 1.16.5***

*THE RANDOMIZER WAS TESTED WITH PYTHON 3.9*

INSTRUCTIONS:
	Move this directory to the datapacks folder of your minecraft world, run the .bat file, wait for it to finish and reload your world.

.bat FILES:
	start-loot-randomizer.bat:
		Randomizes the loot.
	reset-loot-randomizer.bat:
		Resets the loot. (Back to vanilla settings)
